THE PROBLEM

Everyone has ideas. Everyone wants to turn those ideas into something real. You probably have a handful of great ideas even now as you read this (you probably also have some bad ones). Why then is it that so few people realize their imaginative ideas in the real world? What gets lost in the shuffle from imagination to realization? The answer is simple: entrepreneurship, as it exists now, is bloody difficult. It’s difficult to stay motivated, it’s difficult to manage distractions, it’s difficult to find good people to help you along, it’s difficult to find helpful guidance, and it’s especially difficult to steer things once you run into roadblocks and setbacks. The reasons for these difficulties are numerous, but as daunting as they are, they are not insurmountable. Through careful planning, goal setting, selective collaboration, and constant, honest re-appraisals of your project, we can boil the whole process down to the idea-realization resin: effort.

WHAT IS FLO~?

So what the heck are we anyway, and how do we expect to tackle such an elusive problem? In short, we are an entrepreneurial platform that promotes the growth and realization of ideas through complementary and productive collaboration, crowd-sourced feedback, and frank self-evaluation - all in one place. If this sounds a little familiar, it should. There are lots of other great outlets for attempting to tackle work-flow - a fundamental part of the entrepreneurial path. These other websites or applications often cover different points of the process. (for a more detailed breakdown of the FLO `recipe, click here) We feel that by combining the strengths of our predecessors with some things that haven’t yet been addressed, we can create something better and more impactful.

ENTER FLO~

Nothing comes easily, or without effort. We’ve heard this re-iterated in various forms since, well, ever. We’re here to make the whole thing easier, and to make you get the most out of the effort you put in. Unfortunately, there is no magic wand to make your idea a success overnight - that would be too easy. However, if we focus that effort through the right channels, and leverage our power smartly, we can accomplish amazing things. Through our intuitive interface, unique collaboration process and deeply thought-out leveling system, we will help you realize your concept faster than you ever thought possible. Once it gets there, that’s still up to you, but the experience, knowledge and network you gain from using the FLO~ system will prepare your concept for that big step into the real world. Let’s create something beautiful, together.

WHERE ARE WE NOW?

Since spring of last year, we’ve been working exhaustively on finishing our prototype. If you’re interested in taking a sneak-peak, and seeing how the skeleton looks, feel free to email us or message us on Facebook and we’ll gladly show you how it works! Currently, we are in the coding phase of our Beta version, which we hope to have completed by April. If you have any feedback or questions of any kind, please share them with us, we’d be happy to hear from you!

WHAT'S NEXT?

Once we have the Beta up and running, we will start encountering bugs...probably lots of them. This will be a critical time for us, and feedback from you will be crucial. We will also be running a survey shortly afterwards, to see what you like, and what you don’t. Until then, Start. Build. Lead. Grow.
