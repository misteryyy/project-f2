THE FLO~ RECIPE

Like our predecessors, we believe that there’s something missing on the market - a piece of the puzzle that just doesn’t fit, or is somehow incomplete. Having said that, we are also acutely aware that without the diligence and ingenuity of those that came before us, we wouldn’t be anywhere near where we are today.

The FLO~ recipe is not an exact one. For this reason we feel that it’s easiest to understand what we’re about through comparisons to existing concepts.

  • with 'Quirky' we share the philosophy of “getting things done”. We share their belief in creating a stream-lined and repeatable process/checklist to success. We also share the reliance on a powerful, cohesive, self-sustainable and like-minded community.
  • with 'Behance' we share the belief that entrepreneurship can be cultivated and fostered
  • with 'Kickstarter' we share the focus and reliance on the power of large, scalable and interested crowds
  • with 'Ahhha' and 'StumbleUpon' we share the intuitive, easy flow and dissemination of ideas and mix that with the powerful complementary function of the unexpected collision of those ideas
  • with 'LinkedIn' we share the concept that social connections mark the starting point for any collaborative process
  • with 'MeetUp' and 'Start Up Weekend' and 'Grub With Us' we share the belief that like-minded people can pull-together and interact and collaborate like never before!
